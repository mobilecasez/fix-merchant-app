# Privacy Policy for ShopFlix AI

**Last Updated: December 23, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ShopFlix AI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Shopify application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information Provided by Shopify
When you install our App, Shopify provides us with certain information about your store:
- Store name and domain
- Store owner email address
- Product data (titles, descriptions, images, prices, variants)
- Store location information
- API access tokens

### 2. Information You Provide
- Product URLs from external e-commerce platforms (Amazon, eBay, etc.)
- Product customizations and edits you make within the App
- Subscription plan selections and billing information

### 3. Automatically Collected Information
- Usage data (features used, actions taken within the App)
- Device information (browser type, IP address)
- Error logs and performance data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Core Functionality
- Import product data from external platforms
- Generate AI-powered product descriptions
- Create and manage products in your Shopify store
- Process subscription payments
- Track usage limits based on your subscription plan

### Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Debug errors and fix technical issues
- Develop new features and enhancements

### Communication
- Send transactional emails (subscription confirmations, usage warnings)
- Respond to support requests
- Notify you of important updates or changes

### Legal Compliance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights and prevent fraud

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- **Database**: Your subscription and usage data is stored in our secure database
- **Shopify**: Product and store data remains in your Shopify store
- **Temporary**: Scraped product data is processed in memory and not permanently stored

### Security Measures
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted database storage
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure API token management

### Data Retention
- **Active Users**: Data retained while you use the App
- **After Uninstall**: All shop data is automatically deleted within 48 hours (via Shopify's GDPR webhooks)
- **Backups**: May be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery, then permanently deleted

## Shopify-Specific Information

### App Uninstall
When you uninstall the App:
- We receive an uninstall webhook from Shopify immediately
- Your subscription data is marked for deletion
- Within 48 hours, Shopify sends a shop/redact webhook (GDPR requirement)
- Upon receiving shop/redact, all your data is immediately and permanently deleted
- Backups may retain data for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

### Data Access
We only access Shopify data that you explicitly grant permissions for during installation.

### API Usage
All API calls are logged for security and debugging purposes. Logs are retained for 90 days.
