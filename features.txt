# Future Features

- AI-Powered Product Creation from URL:
  - Create a new product form similar to the Shopify Product Add Form.
  - The form will have a field for the user to enter a URL from any e-commerce website.
  - Use AI to crawl the provided URL and fetch all product details.
  - This includes product title, description, price, variants, and images.
  - The fetched details will be automatically filled into the product form.
  - The user can then review and edit the details before creating the product in their Shopify store.
